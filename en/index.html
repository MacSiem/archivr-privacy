Privacy Policy — Archvr

Last updated: May 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Archvr is a mobile app for packing, unpacking and password-protecting archives (ZIP, 7z, TAR, TAR.GZ, RAR-read). Privacy is foundational to the product: the app runs locally on your device, your files never leave your phone for our servers, and archive passwords are never stored anywhere.

The data controller is Maciej Siemiński (contact: mail@macsiem.dev).

2. What data we process

2.1 Your files and their content

Files, archives and their content selected by you are processed exclusively on your device. The app operator has no access to them, they are never sent to any server or backed up to the cloud. Pack, unpack, add-password and remove-password operations all happen in device memory.

2.2 Archive passwords

Passwords you type to encrypt or decrypt an archive are used only during that specific operation and cleared from memory immediately after it completes. Archvr does not store passwords on the device (not in SecureStore, not in app memory, not in history). Each operation requires re-entering the password.

2.3 Local operation history

The app keeps a local history of recent operations on your device (up to 50 entries) as metadata: archive filename, format, size, operation date, password-used flag (yes/no). The history does not contain passwords, file contents, or archive contents. You can delete a single entry or the entire history at any time in app settings.

2.4 Diagnostic data (Sentry)

To detect crashes and improve app stability, we use Sentry (sentry.io). Sentry collects:

  • error stack traces (crash logs);
  • device information (model, OS version, locale);
  • app version and build number.

Sentry does not collect file names, archive contents, passwords or operation history. Diagnostic data is retained for up to 90 days.

2.5 Advertising (AdMob)

Archvr's free tier displays non-intrusive banner and interstitial ads after pack/unpack operations. Ads are not shown on Premium screens, during onboarding or while a file operation is in progress. Ads are served by Google AdMob, whose SDK:

  • collects an advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID / Apple IDFA), shared with Google for ad delivery;
  • collects diagnostic device information (model, country-level location, language).

By default we serve non-personalized ads. You can reset your advertising identifier at any time in Android or iOS settings. You can disable ads with the in-app one-time purchase „Lifetime — Remove Ads" or with the „Annual — Remove Ads" subscription.

We never sell personal data to third parties.

2.6 In-app purchases (IAP)

Payments and subscription renewals are handled exclusively by Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). Archvr does not process or store credit card details, billing addresses or other payment specifics. The app keeps locally only the purchase status (e.g. „premium active", „subscription active until ").

2.7 Device permissions

  • Files / Documents: access to files you select for packing/unpacking (iOS: NSDocumentsFolderUsageDescription; Android: scoped storage, no separate permission).
  • Photo library (optional): pack photos from your library (iOS: NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription; Android: READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, READ_MEDIA_VIDEO).
  • App Tracking Transparency (iOS): consent prompt for ad tracking (NSUserTrackingUsageDescription) — you can decline; ads still work but as non-personalized.
  • Android Advertising ID: com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID.

Each permission is requested in context and you can decline it without losing access to core app functionality.

3. Legal basis (GDPR)

For users in the European Union:

  • contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) — core app functionality;
  • legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — Sentry diagnostics, non-personalized AdMob ads;
  • consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) — iOS App Tracking Transparency (you can decline; non-personalized ads are the default).

4. Your rights (GDPR)

For data processed by Archvr and third parties (Sentry, Google AdMob, Apple, Google Play) you have the right to:

  • access your data;
  • rectification;
  • erasure („right to be forgotten");
  • restriction of processing;
  • data portability;
  • object to processing;
  • withdraw consent at any time (where processing was based on consent);
  • lodge a complaint with your data protection authority.

To exercise your rights with Archvr, contact: mail@macsiem.dev. Sentry diagnostic data will be deleted upon your request within 30 days. Local operation history is fully under your control — delete it in app settings or uninstall the app to remove all local data.

5. Children

Archvr is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children below this age. If a parent/guardian notices that a child used the app and their data was collected, please contact: mail@macsiem.dev — we will delete the data promptly.

6. Third parties

  • Google AdMob (Google LLC) — ads and advertising identifier. Policy: policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Sentry (Functional Software, Inc.) — diagnostics. Policy: sentry.io/privacy.
  • Apple App Store (Apple Inc.) — iOS distribution and payments. Policy: apple.com/legal/privacy.
  • Google Play (Google LLC) — Android distribution and payments. Policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

7. Security

Archvr uses the AES-256 standard for encrypting ZIP and 7z archives with passwords. Passwords are not persisted on the device. All file operations happen locally, in the app's sandbox.

The app does not require an account, login or internet connection to perform pack, unpack or password management operations (apart from ad fetching and occasional Sentry diagnostic requests).

8. Policy changes

This policy may be updated as the app evolves. Material changes are communicated in-app and on the Apple App Store / Google Play listings. The last update date is shown at the top of this page.
